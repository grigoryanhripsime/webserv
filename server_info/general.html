The Three Little Pigs

A classic fairy tale of cleverness and courage

Once Upon a Time

Three little pigs left home to build houses of their own. The first pig built a house of straw, the second used sticks, and the third—being the wisest—chose bricks.

The Straw House

The first little pig quickly built a straw house. But when the Big Bad Wolf came and asked to come in, the pig refused. So the wolf huffed and puffed—and blew the house down!

The Stick House

The pig ran to his brother’s stick house. But again the wolf huffed and puffed—and the stick house fell too! Both pigs ran to the third pig’s brick house.

The Brick House

At the brick house, the wolf huffed and puffed but couldn’t blow it down. He tried climbing in through the chimney—but the pigs had a pot of boiling water waiting. The wolf fled and never returned.

Happily Ever After

The three pigs learned the value of hard work and lived happily ever after in the strong brick house.
